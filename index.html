Skoda Octavia facelift
Rs. 35.00 - 40.00 Lakh Estimated Price
Dec 2024 (Tentative) Expected Launch

The changes on the exterior include a slightly redesigned signature radiator grille surrounded by a new pair of LED headlamps with Matrix technology along with integrated LED DRLs. At the rear, will get completely new LED taillamps with a new bumper that has sharper cuts and creases. The silhouette of the car will remain almost identical to the outgoing model apart from the optional freshly designed 19-inch alloy wheels. Now, coming to the inside, the 2024 Skoda Octavia will come equipped with a 13-inch touchscreen infotainment screen with mobile connectivity, dual-zone climate control, USB type-C charger, and keyless entry/exit. Other notable changes could include a dual-tone black-brown interior theme, rear window blinds, ergonomic seats, and electronic parking brakes.
